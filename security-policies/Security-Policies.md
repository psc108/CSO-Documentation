## Policy Governance

### Policy Development Process
1. **Needs Assessment**: Identify policy requirements
2. **Stakeholder Consultation**: Gather input from relevant teams
3. **Draft Development**: Create initial policy draft
4. **Review and Approval**: Management and legal review
5. **Implementation**: Deploy and communicate policy
6. **Monitoring**: Track compliance and effectiveness

### Policy Review Cycle
- **Annual Review**: Comprehensive policy assessment
- **Triggered Reviews**: Based on regulatory changes or incidents
- **Continuous Monitoring**: Ongoing compliance tracking
- **Update Process**: Version control and change management

### Policy Approval Authority
- **CISO**: Security policies and standards
- **CTO**: Technology and infrastructure policies
- **Legal**: Regulatory and compliance policies
- **CEO**: Enterprise-wide strategic policies

## Policy Implementation

### Communication Strategy
- Policy publication and distribution
- Training and awareness programs
- Regular reminders and updates
- Feedback collection and incorporation

### Compliance Monitoring
- Automated policy compliance checking
- Regular audit and assessment
- Exception tracking and management
- Corrective action planning

### Enforcement Mechanisms
- Disciplinary procedures for violations
- System-based controls and restrictions
- Regular compliance reporting
- Escalation procedures
